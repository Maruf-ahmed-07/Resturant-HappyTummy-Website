The Happy Tummy Vibe

Fresh, Homemade, and Crafted With Love

At Happy Tummy, we believe that fast food doesn't mean compromising on quality. We pride ourselves on offering food that feels and tastes genuinely homemade.

Nothing sits under a heat lamp here. Every single dish is made on the spot right after you place your order. Our live kitchen concept means you can smell the aromas, hear the sizzle, and witness the passion that goes into your meal.

Whether you're dropping by to pick up your favorite Meatbox or staying for a bite, our space offers a really cozy vibe that makes you feel right at home in West Rampura.
